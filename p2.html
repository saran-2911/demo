About us

Biryani is a beloved and aromatic dish that originated in South Asia, known for its rich flavors and vibrant colors. It typically consists of layers of marinated meat (like chicken, beef, lamb, or goat) or vegetables, cooked with fragrant basmati rice, and seasoned with a blend of spices such as saffron, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon.
